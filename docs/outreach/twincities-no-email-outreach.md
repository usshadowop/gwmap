# Twin Cities — outreach for stores with no email

For the Twin Cities stores that have **no public email** — use these to paste into
the store's website contact form, or to read from / send the link when you call.
Each block has the store's contact channel(s), its pre-filled verification-form
link, a copy-paste message, and a short phone script.

`#` matches [`twincities-no-email-stores.csv`](twincities-no-email-stores.csv).
Confirmed stores (Lewis = loyalty, Level Up = 10%) use "verify your details"
wording; unconfirmed stores use "confirm your listing" wording (they're hidden on
the map until confirmed). Sign every message as `Jon@warhammerstores.com`.
